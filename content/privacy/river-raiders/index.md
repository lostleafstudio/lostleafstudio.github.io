# Privacy Policy - River Raiders

Effective Date: *January 1, 2026*

Lost Leaf Studio, LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game River Raiders handles user information. River Raiders is an offline arcade-style game and is designed to operate without collecting or transmitting personal data.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal or non-personal data.
This includes, but is not limited to:

- Names, email addresses, phone numbers
- Device identifiers
- Location information
- Gameplay analytics
- Contact lists, photos, or files
- Financial information

## Third-Party Services

River Raiders uses no third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.
The app does not:

- Show ads
- Track users for advertising
- Connect to external servers
- Use social media integrations

## In-App Purchases

The app contains no in-app purchases or payment features.

## Internet Connectivity

River Raiders operates entirely offline. The app does not require or use an internet connection and does not send any data outside the device.

## Children’s Privacy

River Raiders collects no data from any user, including children under 13. The game is compliant with COPPA, GDPR-K, and similar regulations because no personal information is gathered.

## Your Rights

Since no data is collected or stored:

- We have no data to access, modify, or delete.
- No data requests are necessary.

## Security

Because River Raiders does not collect or transmit information, no user data is at risk of unauthorized access or disclosure.

## Changes to This Policy

If future updates introduce features that require data collection or third-party services, this Privacy Policy will be updated before release.
